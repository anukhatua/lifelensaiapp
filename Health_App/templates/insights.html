🌿 Wellness Hub

Small habits shape long-term health. Focus on consistency, not extremes.

🚶 Movement

6k–10k steps/day. Break long sitting. Movement boosts energy & heart health.

🔥 Energy Balance

1800–2200 kcal (sedentary), 2200–2800+ (active). Match intake with activity.

💧 Hydration

2–3L daily. Supports metabolism, focus, and reduces fatigue.

😴 Sleep

7–9 hours daily. Consistent timing improves recovery & performance.

❤️ Heart Signals

Normal resting BPM: 60–100. High trends may indicate stress or low recovery.

⚖️ Lifestyle

Limit smoking & alcohol. Maintain routine. Small habits → big impact.

🍎 Daily Diet Guide

🌅 Morning

Warm water (optional lemon) for hydration & digestion.

🍳 Breakfast

Protein + carbs (eggs, oats, poha). Keeps energy stable.

🍛 Lunch

Balanced meal: carbs + protein + vegetables.

☕ Snacks

Fruits or light snacks. Avoid processed food.

🍽️ Dinner

Light & early. Eat 2–3 hrs before sleep.
